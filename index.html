Paul Crosby

Capital One Presentation

Introduction

I was a financial executive that had been working in the city for 20 years. I was mainly involved in credit, trading and broking in the Fixed Income world. One day when the market was quiet, I decided to play a prank on a friend who was looking to open an online shop. I tried to build a website with joke pictures and items on it. I guess the joke is on me now!!! With this came the urge and desire to build websites along with the thrill of having something tangible to show for it as opposed to the paper money that I’d been dealing during my career in finance.

Why Web Development?

I consider myself the “KID at Christmas” at the moment. Everything seems so new and exciting , entering into a new world of web development. Although this is different, in a way it is similar. Every trade was different, every position different. Dealing with personalities from a Sales point of view was different. All a sense of problem solving. Now building websites bring another puzzle to solve.


What is Web Development and what does it mean to me?

The work involved in developing a website for the internet or the intranet . Nowadays it seems to be more about Content Management Systems which acts as the middleware between databases and the user through a browser. To me this is built through the whole company and with an agile process. So all divisions have input and is relevant to everyone. Similar. Building something for the company or the outside world , making it user friendly, interactive, addictive and fun. Keeping up with new trends, new languages and trying to make things as current as possible. Learning and developing new trends and languages. Always experimenting and trying new things.
